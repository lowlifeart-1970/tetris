HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE:

Clear horizontal lines of blocks by filling them completely. Don't let the blocks stack up to the top!

CONTROLS:

  • ← : Move Left
  • → : Move Right
  • ↓ : Soft Drop
  • ↑ or SPACE : Rotate
  • P : Pause / Resume
